Tab 16

Explore the full relationship graph of any Salesforce SObject. For a selected object, Tab 16 queries all parent and child references — Lookup, Master-Detail, and Hierarchical — and displays each relation with its cascade-delete and restricted-delete behaviour. Filters let you hide standard relations, standard user relations, parent-only or child-only links. Results can be exported to Excel for documentation or impact analysis.
